About Me

Hello! My Name is John

I am a Software Developer based in Atlanta, Ga. Before I went into Software Development, I graduated from college with a degree in Civil Engineering. Not knowing what the future lies and wondering if I'm heading down the right path, I soon discovered my passion for coding. It all started when one of my colleague designed a visual novel with a dreadful ending. I soon decided to change that ending through code to make it a little bit more intense and interesting. Coding had showed me to running into many brick walls (head first) and turning around to finding the right path. I soon decided to enrolled in an immersive coding bootcamp called DigitalCrafts in summer 2019 to broaden my knowledge and enhanced my coding skills.

Outside of coding, I draw manga characters, read visual novels, watch anime, play video games, travel, and play basketball.

Marvel Weather

Planet of the Shapes

Planet of the Shapes

GITHUB Live Site

    Type: (Group Project) | Github | Live Site

  • Description

    • A Back-end project that allows users to login and register into the site to play single and multiplayer game modes and keep track of their high scores. In single player mode, the user starts off at level 1 and killing off opposing enemy shapes to stack up points to advance to higher levels. In multiplayer mode, it allows a user to chat with other users. Each user can operate their own shape and move around the screen.

  • My Role

    • I was responsible for single player mode such as using canvas for gameplay, implemented music and sound effects, and storing the data for the users high scores.

  • Built With

    • HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, Canvas, Express, PostgreSQL, Socket io.
